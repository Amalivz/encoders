Gavi


Team: FC Barcelona
Jersey Number: 30
Position: Mid-Fielder


A combative and technically gifted midfielder Pablo Páez Gavira, better known as 'Gavi', was born in the town
of Los Palacios y Villafranca near Seville. As a youngster he made his way to the youth system at Real Betis
where he spent two seasons representing the verdiblancos at the Luis del Sol training complex just a stone's
throw from the Benito Villamarín stadium.


Career

Gavi was born in Los Palacios y Villafranca, Andalusia.[1] He started his career at La Liara Balompié, a club
in his hometown, where he spent two years, between 2010 and 2012.[6][7] From there he moved on to Real
Betis's youth academy, where he spent two seasons.[8][9] He scored 95 goals for the youth team of Real
Betis.[10]
