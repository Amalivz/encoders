Ronald Araujo

Team: FC Barcelona
Jersey Number: 4
Position: Defender


Ronald Araujo was born in Rivera, Uruguay, on 7 March 1999. The young central defender began playing
professionally for his hometown team, and later joined the lower categories at Rentistas in the Uruguayan
Second Division.


Career

Ronald Araujo is a professional player who is currently playing as a defender for Barcelona Football club and
Uruguay National team.
Ronald Araujo also had a successful career spell with his first two club, Rentistas and Boston River, where he
played a total of 110 games and scored 17 goals (all for Rentistas).
Ronald Araujo has been a player for Uruguay national team both at the youth and senior level. He represented his
country in the FIFA 2018 U20 World Cup and the 2022 World Cup qualifiers.
