Ansu Fati


Team: FC Barcelona
Jersey Number: 10
Position: Forward


Anssumane Fati (31 October 2002) was born in Bissau, the capital of Guinea Bissau. The striker moved to
Seville at the age of six where he began in the youth team set up at Sevilla. In 2012, at the age of 10, he joined
the FC Barcelona youth teams before making his debut with the first team in the 2019/20 season.


Career

After representing local side Herrera and the youth teams of Sevilla, he joined Barcelona's La Masia in 2012,
aged ten,[12][13] a year after his brother had made the same move.[14]
On 24 July 2019, Fati signed his first professional contract with Barcelona, agreeing to a deal until 2022.
[15] On 25 August, before even appearing with the reserves, Fati made his first team and La Liga debut. He came
on as a late-game substitute for goalscorer Carles Pérez in a 5 to 2 home win over Real Betis.[16] Aged 16
years and 298 days, he became the second-youngest player to debut for the club, only 18 days older than
Vicenç Martínez in 1941.[17]
