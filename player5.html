Sergio Busquets

Team: FC Barcelona
Jersey Number: 5
Position: Mid-Fielder


Born in Sabadell, he joined the Barça youth system in the summer of 2005 from Jabac. That season, playing
for the U19A side, he won three major trophies alongside the likes of Bojan Krkic, Giovani, Jeffren and Marc
Crosas.


Career

Busquets made his senior international debut for Spain in April 2009, and has since made 143 appearances
for the national side. He helped the country win the 2010 World Cup and Euro 2012 tournaments, and also
featured at three other World Cups and two European Championships. He retired from international football
following the 2022 World Cup.
